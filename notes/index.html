Notes on trust.

Six short pieces, written while building with AI agents day to day. Five share one throughline — the gap between what's visible on the surface and what's actually doing the work underneath, and the discipline of trusting nothing you can't verify. The sixth is a practical trainer that happened to land here too. Read in order, or skip around — none of them require each other.
